GINATAANG TULINGAN

Ginataang Tulingan is one of the favorite dish of the author because they live near the fishport and it is like a common dish served everyday. And the taste uniquely stays from the mouth.

Ginataang Tulingan is a Filipino dish made with mackerel tuna cooked in coconut milk along with spices and vegetables. "Ginataan" refers to dishes cooked with coconut milk, while "Tulingan" refers to the type of mackerel used in this recipe.
The dish typically includes ingredients like ginger, garlic, onion, chili peppers, eggplant, and sometimes bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) for additional flavor.
The mackerel tuna is usually simmered in the coconut milk until tender, absorbing the rich flavors of the sauce. It's a popular and flavorful dish enjoyed in the Philippines, especially in coastal regions where fish is abundant.
